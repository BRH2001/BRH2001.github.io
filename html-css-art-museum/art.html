Starry Night

Vincent van Gogh (CSS interpretation)

Vincent van Gogh (1853-1890) was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter. Starry Night is one of his most famous works, painted in 1889. It depicts the view from his asylum room window just before sunrise.

Composition with Red, Blue and Yellow

Piet Mondrian (CSS interpretation)

Piet Mondrian (1872-1944) was a Dutch painter and theoretician. He is regarded as one of the greatest artists of the 20th century. His non-representational paintings he called "compositions" used only primary colors against black and white.

Circles within Circles

Wassily Kandinsky (CSS interpretation)

Wassily Kandinsky (1866-1944) was a Russian painter and art theorist. He is credited with painting one of the first purely abstract works. Kandinsky believed circles represented cosmic harmony and used them extensively in his work.
